# SleepWhisper: Night Recorder – Support

Welcome to the official support page for **SleepWhisper: Night Recorder**.

SleepWhisper helps you record your nights and detect sound events while you sleep.  
The app stores data only on your device and does not upload your audio recordings to any servers.

---

## Contact & Support

If you have any questions, issues, or feature requests, you can reach out in these ways:

1. **GitHub Issues**  
   - Open an issue in this repository and describe your problem or suggestion.  
   - Please include your device model and iOS version if you are reporting a bug.

2. **From the App**  
   - Open *SleepWhisper*  
   - Go to **Settings → About / Support**  
   - Follow the instructions shown there to contact the developer or send feedback.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does SleepWhisper upload my recordings to the cloud?**  
A: No. All recordings and sound data stay on your device. They are not sent to external servers.

**Q: I found a bug. What should I do?**  
A: Please open a GitHub issue in this repository with a short description, steps to reproduce the problem, and your device/iOS information.

**Q: How can I request a new feature?**  
A: You can suggest features via GitHub issues. Mark your issue as a *feature request*.

---

## Privacy

SleepWhisper is designed to be as private as possible:

- Audio recordings are stored locally on your device.
- No automatic account creation is required.
- Analytics and diagnostics are limited to what is necessary to keep the app running reliably.

For more details, please refer to the app’s Privacy Policy inside the App Store listing.
